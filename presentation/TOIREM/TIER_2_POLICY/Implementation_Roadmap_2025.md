## ‚ö†Ô∏è RISK MITIGATION

### Identified Risks & Mitigation:

1. **Risk**: Overwhelming demand exceeds budget
   - **Mitigation**: Set monthly caps, maintain waitlist

2. **Risk**: Fraudulent applications
   - **Mitigation**: Verification procedures, reference checks

3. **Risk**: Staff overwhelm during launch
   - **Mitigation**: Staggered intake, volunteer support

4. **Risk**: Documentation errors risk CRA compliance
   - **Mitigation**: Double-check system, regular audits

5. **Risk**: Privacy breach of applicant data
   - **Mitigation**: Locked storage, password protection, staff training
