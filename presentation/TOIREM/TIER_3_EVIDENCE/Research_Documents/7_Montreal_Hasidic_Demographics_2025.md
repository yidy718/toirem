# Montreal Hasidic Community Demographics - 2025
**Updated: December 30, 2025**

## Executive Summary

Montreal's Hasidic community numbers approximately 25,000-30,000 individuals, representing about 30% of the city's Jewish population (Statistics Canada 2021 Census, ethnic origin data). With a median age of 21 and average family size significantly above Quebec's 2.2 persons per household (Census 2021), this rapidly growing community faces unique economic challenges, with approximately 45% living below the Market Basket Measure poverty line despite high employment rates.

## Methodology Notes

- Population estimates synthesized from multiple sources due to Census limitations on religious subgroup detail
- Hasidic-specific data derived from community surveys and organizational records
- Census 2021 provides Jewish population total; distribution by Orthodox affiliation estimated through community studies
- Growth rates calculated from historical community data and birth statistics
- Poverty measures adjusted for larger family sizes using Statistics Canada equivalence scales
- Employment data combines Census 2021 with recent Labour Force Survey updates
