## CALCULATION EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Under $5,000
Family of 6 needs $3,000 for rent
- Check: Under $5,000? YES
- Process: SIMPLIFIED
- Verify: ID and declaration only
- Approve: Manager signature
- Pay: Direct to landlord

### Example 2: Over $5,000 (60% Formula)
Family of 8 requests $25,000
- Annual costs: $168,000 (from table)
- Verified income: $110,000 (both NOAs)
- Deficit: $58,000
- 60% of deficit: $34,800
- Can provide up to: $34,800 (if requested $25,000, approve $25,000)

### Example 3: Over $5,000 (Limited by Request)
Family of 6 requests $10,000
- Annual costs: $134,400
- Verified income: $90,000
- Deficit: $44,400
- 60% of deficit: $26,640
- Can provide up to: $26,640 (but only approve $10,000 requested)
