## 1.3 Charitable Objects

As registered with the Canada Revenue Agency, our charitable purposes are:

1. **To relieve poverty** by providing financial assistance to individuals and families in need

2. **To provide basic necessities of life** including food, shelter, clothing, and medical care to those who cannot afford them

3. **To support educational opportunities** for children from low-income families

4. **To assist with emergency financial needs** arising from unforeseen circumstances that place individuals and families in financial distress

## 1.4 Geographic Scope

Primary Service Area: Greater Montreal, Quebec, Canada
- Focus: Jewish Hasidic communities
- Coverage: Montreal Island and surrounding areas
