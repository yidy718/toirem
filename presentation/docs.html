Master Formula

Annual Threshold =
(Adults × $24,143) +
(Children × Child Cost) +
Housing Cost +
Religious Obligations ($15,966) +
Special Adjustments

Component Breakdown:

  • Child Cost: $8,500 base + ($500 × school age children)
  • Housing Cost: $24,000 base + ($6,000 × number of children)
  • Religious Obligations: $15,966 per family (fixed)
  • Special Adjustments: Medical needs, disabilities, etc.
