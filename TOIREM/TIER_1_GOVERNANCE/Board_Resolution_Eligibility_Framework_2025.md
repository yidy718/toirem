## RESOLUTION TO ADOPT FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE ELIGIBILITY FRAMEWORK

**WHEREAS** Faire un Don Canada / Toirem Canada (the "Charity") was registered with the Canada Revenue Agency as a registered charity effective January 1, 2025, under registration number 751018425RR0001;

**WHEREAS** the Charity's charitable purposes include the relief of poverty by providing financial assistance to individuals and families in need;

**WHEREAS** the Canada Revenue Agency requires registered charities engaged in poverty relief to establish clear eligibility criteria and maintain proper documentation demonstrating that funds are used for their intended charitable purposes;

**WHEREAS** the Board of Directors recognizes the need for a comprehensive framework to ensure consistent, fair, and compliant distribution of charitable resources;

**WHEREAS** the proposed Eligibility Framework has been developed based on:
- Current Montreal cost of living data for 2025
- Statistics Canada's Low Income Measure (LIM)
- Canada Revenue Agency guidelines for poverty relief charities
- Best practices in charitable sector governance;

**WHEREAS** the Board has reviewed the following documents:
1. Toirem Canada Financial Assistance Eligibility Framework (dated January 1, 2025)
2. Quick Reference Guide for Staff
3. Income Assessment Worksheet
4. Montreal Cost of Living Analysis 2025;

**NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED THAT:**

---

## 1. ADOPTION OF FRAMEWORK

The Board of Directors hereby approves and adopts the **Toirem Canada Financial Assistance Eligibility Framework** effective January 1, 2025, including:

a) **Income eligibility thresholds** as follows:
   - Single person: $35,000 annual maximum
   - Couple: $49,000 annual maximum
   - Family of 3: $60,000 annual maximum
   - Family of 4: $72,000 annual maximum
   - Additional family members: +$12,000 per person

b) **Assistance categories and limits** as outlined in Section 3 of the Framework

c) **Documentation requirements** as specified in Section 4 of the Framework

d) **Special population adjustments** for seniors, persons with disabilities, single parents, new immigrants, and students

---

## 2. DELEGATION OF AUTHORITY

The Board hereby delegates authority for assistance approvals as follows:

a) **Staff Members** may approve assistance up to $500 per request
b) **Executive Director/Manager** may approve assistance from $501 to $2,500
c) **Board Approval Required** for all assistance exceeding $2,500
d) **Emergency Override Authority** granted to Executive Director for time-sensitive cases under $1,000

---

## 3. IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

The Board directs management to:

a) **Train all staff and volunteers** on the Framework within 30 days
b) **Implement tracking systems** for all disbursements and recipient information
c) **Establish vendor relationships** for direct payment arrangements
d) **Create secure filing systems** for confidential applicant documentation
e) **Develop monthly reporting templates** for Board review

---

## 4. COMPLIANCE AND OVERSIGHT

The Board commits to:

a) **Monthly review** of disbursement reports
b) **Quarterly review** of Framework effectiveness
c) **Annual review and update** of income thresholds and assistance limits
d) **Random audits** of 10% of approved applications monthly
e) **Annual third-party review** of compliance procedures

---

## 5. REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

Management shall provide the Board with:

a) **Monthly reports** including:
   - Number of applications received, approved, and denied
   - Total disbursements by category
   - Geographic distribution of recipients
   - Average assistance per household

b) **Quarterly reports** including:
   - Comparison to budget
   - Trends in assistance requests
   - Success stories and outcomes
   - Any compliance concerns

c) **Annual report** for T3010 filing including:
   - Total charitable disbursements
   - Number of beneficiaries served
   - Demonstration of public benefit
   - Compliance with disbursement quota

---

## 6. BUDGET ALLOCATION

The Board approves the following initial budget allocations for 2025:

a) **Total assistance budget:** $________________
b) **Emergency fund reserve:** $________________
c) **Administrative costs:** Not to exceed 15% of assistance provided

---

## 7. AMENDMENTS

This Framework may be amended by:
a) **Minor adjustments** (under 10% change in limits) by Executive Director with Board notification
b) **Major amendments** require full Board approval by resolution
c) **Annual review** mandatory each December for following year adjustments

---

## 8. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Resolution and the adopted Framework shall take effect immediately upon passage and shall remain in force until amended or replaced by subsequent Board resolution.
