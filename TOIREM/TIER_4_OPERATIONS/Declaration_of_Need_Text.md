## IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### For Staff:

1. **Under $5,000:** Only the basic declaration is needed - no income documents
2. **$5,000 and over:** Extended declaration PLUS income verification required
3. **Language:** Offer form in language of choice (English, French, or Yiddish)
4. **Witnessing:** Staff should witness the signature
5. **Dating:** Ensure declaration is dated same day as application

### Legal Protection:

The declaration serves as:
- Legal attestation of need
- Authorization for assistance
- Protection against fraud
- CRA compliance documentation

### Key Principle:

**For amounts under $5,000, this signed declaration IS the income verification.** The applicant's signature attesting to their need replaces the requirement for tax returns, pay stubs, or bank statements.
