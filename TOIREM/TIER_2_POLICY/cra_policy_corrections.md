```
## 2. JUSTIFICATION FOR SIMPLIFIED PROCESS (Under $5,000)

### CRA's Risk-Based Framework

CRA Guidance CG-029 (October 2024) establishes that documentation requirements should be proportionate to risk. CG-029 states that a charity "may need to apply selection criteria and processes" based on four factors:
1. The **value** of the charitable benefit
2. The **frequency** at which it is provided
3. The **location** where it is provided
4. The charity's **operational circumstances**

For low-value, low-frequency benefits to populations unlikely to include those not in need, CG-029 explicitly states: "The potential of providing non-incidental private benefit is negligible."

### THE CORNERSTONE: Signed Declaration of Need

For amounts under $5,000, we accept the applicant's signed declaration stating "I need financial assistance for my personal day-to-day needs" as sufficient verification of poverty.
```
