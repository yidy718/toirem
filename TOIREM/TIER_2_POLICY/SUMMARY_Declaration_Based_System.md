For assistance requests **under $5,000**, Toirem Canada uses a **declaration-based system** where the applicant's signature on a statement of need replaces the requirement for income documentation.

## THE DECLARATION

### What Applicants Sign:

### What This Signature Means:

- Commitment that information is truthful
- Authorization for assistance

## WHY THIS WORKS

### 1. Cultural Context
In the Hasidic community, one's word and signature carry sacred weight. False declarations would result in severe community consequences beyond any legal penalties.

### 2. Practical Reality
Families with 6-8 children facing eviction don't have time to gather months of documentation. The declaration allows immediate response to urgent needs.

### 3. Observable Verification
Staff can verify need through:
- Family size (visible)
- Housing conditions (observable)
- Community knowledge (rabbinical awareness)
- Request patterns (basic necessities only)

### 4. Natural Limits
- Maximum $5,000 per request
- Category caps (rent $1,500/month, food $500/month)
- Annual family limits
- All payment methods acceptable (check, e-transfer, vendor, gift card)
